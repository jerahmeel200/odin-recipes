Pasta Recipes

Recipie Description

This homemade pasta recipe is consistently great and easy to make with flour, eggs, salt, olive oil, and water.

Roll out by hand or use a pasta machine to cut dough into desired pasta shape.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Starts with two cups of all-purpose flour.
  2. Add Eggs to lend fat and moisture to the pasta dough.
  3. Add two tablespoons of olive oil add moisture and help the dough come together.
  4. Add A teaspoon of salt takes the flavor up a notch.
  5. Add just enough water to form a smooth, thick dough.
