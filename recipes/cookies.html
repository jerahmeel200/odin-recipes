Recipie Description

These molasses cookies are soft and chewy. This is my mom's recipe and one of my favorites.

They store well and can be frozen. Great for gifting during the holidays!

Ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Molasses
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Spices
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Mix a cup of sugar, the butter, and the egg together. Stir in the molasses.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a separate bowl, then stir it into the wet mixture.
  3. Roll the dough into balls and roll each ball in the remaining sugar.
  4. Bake until the tops are cracked and cool on wire racks.
